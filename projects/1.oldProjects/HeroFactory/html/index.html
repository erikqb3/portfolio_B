  • About

    Hero Factory is a website where artists who strive to create wholesome, uplifting, and awesome art can connect with other such artists. Under the creation of Kire L. Elrid, Hero Factory is similar to that of a job board; where people can post project ideas they want to carry out and those looking for work and experience can offer their service.

    If you have trouble coming up with a good idea of your own, you can seek out someone who needs your skill set and gain experience from their project. If you have an idea but are unable to get help from experts, there are lots of artists in the world who have potential waiting to be unlocked.

    Hero Factory is where artists help each other create heroes that can make the world a better place in terms of art, entertainment, and media!

  • Project Proposals

    Laid out in the format of a discussion board, participants can give a brief summary of an idea they have for a project and list off the kind of experienced individuals they need in order to make their idea a reality. If you're an aspiring artist who wants a taste of what it's like collaborating with others, you can browse the chat to find a project that satisfies your fancy.

    Whether you're an animator planning on making a YouTube video and looking for a voice actor, a writer hoping to publish a novel and looking for an editor and an illustrator, a composer simply trying to beef up his portfolio and looking for a handful of musicians, or anything in between; post your intentions and invitations for others to come help you out!

  • Motivational Resources

    Looking for resources that can help you increase your artistic confidence and hone your skills? Click on the "Motivational Resources" button and find a descripted list of online and physical materials. Such materials involve suggested YouTubers, insightful books, efficient online courses, and preferable software. None of the listed resources can actually be purchased or acquired from this website, but the list and descriptions serves as recommendations. Viewers can provide their comments concerning the recommended resources and even provide recommendations of their own.
